University Feedback and Analysis Portal

🚀 Problem Statement

Universities have multiple services and blocks, such as hostels, libraries, IT services, and medical facilities. Gathering and analyzing feedback from students and faculty is often a tedious and inefficient process. Traditional methods rely on manual surveys and spreadsheet-based analysis, making it difficult to extract meaningful insights.

To solve this challenge, we developed the University Feedback and Analysis Portal – a web-based solution that streamlines feedback collection, data visualization, and decision-making for university administration.

🛠 Tech Stack

✨ Key Features

Stakeholder Feedback Collection – Allows students, faculty, and staff to submit feedback on university services.

Map-Based Interface – Users can select a location and provide feedback specific to that block/service.

Advanced Filtering – Admins can filter feedback based on department, branch, specialization, and date.

Data Export Options – Feedback reports can be downloaded in Excel and CSV formats.

Visual Analytics – Interactive pie charts and bar charts for better understanding of feedback trends.

Leaderboard for Decision-Making – Identifies the best and worst-rated services for improvement.

🔄 How It Works

  1. Users log in and select their role (student, faculty, or staff).
  2. They choose a location (e.g., library, hostel, IT services) using a map-based interface.
  3. Feedback is submitted through a structured form.
  4. Admin accesses the dashboard to view visual insights using charts and graphs.
  5. Admins can filter data, compare trends, and download reports for decision-making.

🎯 Impact & Benefits

  • ✅ Saves time by automating feedback collection.
  • 📊 Provides clear visual insights for better decision-making.
  • 🔍 Enhances accountability and service improvements in universities.
  • 📑 Eliminates manual data entry and spreadsheet-based analysis.
